Play for fun

A simply card game.

Rules :

  1. 30 cards will be displayed on the table, and you can click on one to show it and then found the equal card in the remaining 29.
  2. You have 2 minutes to found all the cards. Timer starts when you click on the first card.
  3. The game ends when time hits 0, or when you've discovered all the same cards.
  4. You can't discover more than one card at time.
